1.Using Google Maps Geocoding API to convert addresses into coordinates.

The Google Geocoding API is a part of Google Maps Platform that converts:

addresses → Coordinates (Geocoding)
Coordinates → Addresses (Reverse Geocoding)

Geocode: Convert a physical address (like a city, street, or landmark) into geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude).

Reverse Geocode: Convert latitude and longitude back into a human-readable address.
